## Key Blog Posts / Articles

### "Is Programmatic SEO Dead?" — SEOmatic
**URL:** https://seomatic.ai/blog/is-programmatic-seo-dead  
**Author:** SEOmatic team  
**Date:** May 2025  
**Why valuable:** Most rigorous post-2024-updates analysis of what pSEO still works vs. what Google now filters. Key distinction: variable injection (dead) vs. dataset-driven differentiation (thriving).

**Key takeaways:**
- March 2024 Core Update explicitly targeted "scaled content abuse"
- Programmatic pages with rich, proprietary datasets survived; thin templates did not
- 4-question test before building any pSEO program: validated demand, sourceable differentiation, winnable SERP, maintenance infrastructure

## Key Research Papers / Data Sources

### BrightEdge AI Search Research (2024)
**Topic:** AI Overviews content selection patterns  
**Key finding:** AI Overviews consistently select content with higher word counts, stronger E-E-A-T signals, and better structured data — not thin programmatic pages.

### SparkToro Zero-Click Research — Rand Fishkin
**URL:** https://sparktoro.com/blog  
**Topic:** Click behavior in AI search era  
**Key finding:** Zero-click searches accelerating; branded search volume becoming a more important proxy for organic impact than session counts.
