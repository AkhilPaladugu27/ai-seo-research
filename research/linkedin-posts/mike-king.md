## Post 2 — How AI Search Actually Works

**Date:** 2025
**Theme:** Technical foundations of AI search (RAG, chunking)

> Most SEOs optimize for a search engine they don't understand.
>
> Here's how AI search actually works:
>
> 1. Your content gets chunked into segments
> 2. Each chunk is embedded as a vector
> 3. When a query comes in, the system retrieves the most relevant chunks
> 4. An LLM synthesizes those chunks into an answer
>
> What this means practically:
> → Headers and structure matter more than ever (they define chunk boundaries)
> → Each section must be independently meaningful
> → Structured data helps the system understand what each chunk is about
> → A page that's great as a whole but weak in sections will lose to a page
>    that's strong at the section level
>
> Optimize for chunks, not pages.

**Key insight:** AI search retrieves content chunks, not full pages. Structure headers well, make each section independently valuable, use structured data.
