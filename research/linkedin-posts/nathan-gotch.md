## Post 1 — AI SEO Workflow That Actually Works

**Date:** 2024
**Theme:** Step-by-step AI content production system

> Here's the exact AI SEO workflow I use for clients:
>
> Step 1: KEYWORD RESEARCH
> → Use Ahrefs to find low-competition, high-intent keywords
> → Cluster by topic, not individual keywords
>
> Step 2: SERP ANALYSIS
> → Manually review top 5 results before writing anything
> → Note: content format, depth, angle, unique data used
>
> Step 3: AI OUTLINE
> → Feed SERP analysis to Claude/ChatGPT
> → Generate outline based on what's ranking + what's missing
>
> Step 4: HUMAN ENHANCEMENT
> → Add first-hand experience, original data, expert quotes
> → This is the E-E-A-T layer AI cannot provide
>
> Step 5: AI DRAFT
> → Use AI to write sections based on enhanced outline
>
> Step 6: HUMAN EDIT
> → Fact-check, add personality, fix AI-isms
>
> Step 7: PUBLISH + INTERNAL LINK
> → Link from 3-5 existing relevant pages immediately
>
> This workflow produces content that ranks AND survives updates.

**Key insight:** 7-step AI SEO workflow — keyword cluster → SERP analysis → AI outline → human enhancement → AI draft → human edit → publish with internal links.

## Post 2 — How to Avoid AI Content Penalties

**Date:** 2024
**Theme:** Google compliance for AI-generated content

> Google doesn't penalize AI content. It penalizes unhelpful content.
>
> The distinction matters.
>
> Content that gets penalized:
> → No original insight (just restates what others said)
> → No author identity (who wrote this and why should I trust them?)
> → No experience signals (has anyone actually done this?)
> → Thin on specifics (vague advice that applies to everything)
> → Factual errors (AI hallucinations left unchecked)
>
> Content that ranks despite being AI-assisted:
> → Has a named expert author with credentials
> → Includes first-hand data or experience
> → Makes specific, verifiable claims
> → Goes deeper than the current top results
> → Has strong internal + external link signals
>
> Use AI to go faster. Use humans to add what AI can't.

**Key insight:** Google targets unhelpful content, not AI content. Penalized = no insight, no author, no experience, vague, errors. Safe = expert author, first-hand data, specific claims, depth.

## Post 3 — Content Clusters vs Individual Pages

**Date:** 2025
**Theme:** Why topic clusters outperform individual optimized pages

> Stop optimizing individual pages. Build topic clusters.
>
> Here's why clusters win in AI search:
>
> A single page on "email marketing" competes with millions of pages.
> A cluster of 15 pages covering every aspect of email marketing
> tells AI: this site owns this topic.
>
> How to build a cluster:
> 1. Pick one broad topic your ICP cares about
> 2. Map every subtopic, question, and use case
> 3. Create a pillar page (comprehensive overview)
> 4. Create spoke pages (deep dives on each subtopic)
> 5. Link pillar ↔ spokes bidirectionally
> 6. Fill gaps until no relevant question goes unanswered
>
> When AI gets asked about your topic,
> it will find your cluster everywhere it looks.

**Key insight:** Topic clusters beat individual pages in AI search. Pillar + spokes + bidirectional linking + gap-filling = site becomes the definitional source on a topic.
