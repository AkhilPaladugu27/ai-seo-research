## Manual Notes (from watching video)

**Key themes covered:**
- Topical authority as a semantic SEO foundation
- How AI systems parse entity relationships in content
- Building content networks (not just pages) for AI comprehension
- AI agents that can identify semantic gaps in topic coverage

**Framework introduced:**
Koray's approach treats a website's content as an interconnected semantic network. Each piece of content should:
1. Define the entity clearly
2. Establish entity attributes
3. Link to related entities
4. Build the "semantic neighborhood" around a topic

**Why this matters for AI SEO content production:**
Traditional SEO produces individual optimized pages.
Semantic SEO (Koray's model) produces topic networks.
AI systems understand networks — they learn from relationships between entities, not just keyword-matched pages.

**Practical implication:**
Before producing AI-powered content at scale, map the semantic network first. Use AI to identify missing nodes in the network, not just to write more content.
