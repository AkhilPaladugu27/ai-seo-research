## How to Find YouTube Video IDs

1. Go to YouTube, search for the expert + topic
2. The video ID is in the URL: `youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
3. Copy the VIDEO_ID into `scripts/fetch_transcripts.py` under the correct expert
